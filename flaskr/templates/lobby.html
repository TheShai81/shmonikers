Rules of Shmonikers

If you've ever played Monikers or Fishbowl, it follows the same rules that you are probably used to.

  • This is best played in 2 competing teams of 2 players each, but this app supports any number of teams with any number of players.
  • Once selecting the appropriate team and game ID, you move to drawing cards.
  • Each player draws (or creates! The app offers both functionalities) 6 cards to submit to the game pool for the entire game. Think about your ability to describe/act out the terms as well as the points associated with each one.
  • For each round, play goes around the table with a randomly generated order until the pool is emptied. The pool is then replenished with the SAME cards for the next round.
  • Round 1: The actor has as many words as they want to describe each term without using words in the term or abbreviations of the term. If you guess the card then you get it! If it's too hard and you want to skip, just press skip and you can come back to it later.
  • Round 2: The actor has only ONE (1) word to describe the term. You can use a different word up to 3 times before you must skip to the next card.
  • Round 3: This round is like charades. The actor can only act out/mime the term. No speaking or sounds.
  • Whichever team has the most points at the end of the game wins!
